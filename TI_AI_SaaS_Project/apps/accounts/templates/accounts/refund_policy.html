Refund Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our service and want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied with X-Crewter, you may be eligible for a refund.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available if you request within 30 days of your initial purchase. To be eligible for a refund, you must have made the purchase in the last 30 days and not have used the service extensively.

Non-Eligible Situations

Refunds are not available for: monthly subscription renewals, annual subscriptions after 6 months of service, or if there is suspected misuse of the platform.

Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved, it will be processed within 5-10 business days. The refund will be credited to the same payment method used for the purchase.

Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at refunds@x-crewter.com with your account information and reason for the refund request.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@x-crewter.com
